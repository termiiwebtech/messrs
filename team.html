Tunde Onakoya, Esq.

Managing Partner – B.SC Economics, LLB (Hons) UNILOND, BL

Tunde Onakoya is a dynamic, warm hearted, friendly, articulate and detailed individual who possesses diverse skill sets that are relevant for today’s fast changing, complex and competition driven professional legal environment. He has penchant for details, hard-work and loyalty. In the last 15 years, he has brought these skills to bear in the effective and efficient management of Messrs Segun and Segun Legal Practitioners, where he now sits as its Managing Partner Diversity and ability to work with teams has helped him to work with people from all works of life. This interaction has effectively enhanced his self-worth in the area of sound interpersonal relationship, business development, strategic thinking, behavioural aspect of business management and managing organizational change

He is passionate about human development and professional ethics. This know-how presently helps his responsibilities and duties at Messrs Segun and Segun as its Managing Partner. He possesses a high level of integrity and cognate Legal experience which allows him to think intuitively, reason logically and able to devise solutions by putting to play various factors and key considerations. He is a member of the Nigerian Bar Association, International Bar Association, Lagos Chamber of commerce and Industry, Nigerian Britain Association and has recently made a foray into Alternative Dispute resolution with a bias for Mediation. Away from Legal Practice, he has an unflinching desire for youth development by disseminating knowledge necessary for empowerment. He sits on the board of a number of youth organisations and companies including, Mindful of Youths Initiative, Mind to Mind Youth Empowerment Limited and Termii Webtech Limited.
