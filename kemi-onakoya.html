KEMI ONAKOYA, Esq.

Associate Partner – LLB (Hons) OAU Ife, BL, LLM

Kemi Onakoya is a law graduate of Obafemi Awolowo University Ife and holds a master’s degree in commercial law from the London School of Economics United Kingdom. She is dual qualified as a Barrister and Solicitor of the Supreme Court of Nigeria and a Solicitor of the Supreme Court of England and has over 25years post qualification experience. She is the Principal Solicitor and Head of the Real Estate and Planning team at Luton Borough Council United Kingdom and acts as the Real Estate Solicitor for London Luton Airport Limited a wholly owned company of Luton borough council. She advises clients across a broad range of industry sectors including large scale developers, investors, pension funds and Housing Associations. She has a wealth of experience of dealing with multi-million pound projects advising on: Commercial Property work including acquisition and disposal of offices, retail and industrial sites including commercial property investment acquisitions and disposals.

Property Development work including development agreements, call options, put options, Pre-emption agreements, conditional contracts, overage agreements, land assembly, highways, water and drainage agreements, planning agreements, environmental and procurement matters. Housing regeneration projects, Private Finance initiatives (PFI) Landlord and Tenant Matters including lease and leasebacks, lease grants, renewals and extensions, advice on rent reviews, break clauses, termination and dilapidations, licenses to assign, change of use, and alterations. Lease surrenders, break clauses and termination of business tenancies Dilapidation issues. property aspects of telecommunications law, Compulsory purchase matters a wide range of commercial, corporate and regulatory matters. secured lending work on behalf of investors and large organisations.
